Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there a Yearly Subscription charge?

The initial cost covers the Lifetime License. The yearly fee of ₹6,000 covers Cloud Server Maintenance (AWS), Daily Data Backups, Technical Support, and Software Updates.

Do I need to buy a biometric device separately?

Yes. FinGold Enterprise supports standard devices like Mantra or Morpho, but the hardware itself must be purchased separately.
