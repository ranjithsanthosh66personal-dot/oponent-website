Common Questions

Why is there a Yearly Subscription charge?

The initial cost covers the Lifetime Software License. The recurring yearly fee of ₹6,000 is strictly for essential ongoing services:

  • Cloud Server Maintenance (AWS)
  • Automated Daily Backups
  • Technical Support
  • Software Updates & Patches
Do I need to buy a biometric device separately?

Yes. The software is fully compatible with industry-standard devices like Mantra or Morpho. However, the physical hardware device must be purchased separately from electronics vendors. We are happy to provide recommendations on compatible models.
